TECHNOLOGY

Technical Indicators

Proprietary Tools for Professional CME Futures Traders

Our proprietary indicators are developed and tested by our team of traders and developers. Built specifically for the NinjaTrader 8 platform, these tools are designed to improve execution, risk clarity, and market context not to replace decision-making.

Indicator 1: Order Levels

Visual Risk & Trade Management on the Chart

The Order Levels indicator visually displays your entry, stop loss, and profit target directly on the chart, allowing you to manage trades with precision and clarity.

Instead of calculating risk mentally or switching between windows, you see exactly how much you are risking and targeting before and during the trade.

Key Features:

  • Displays entry, stop loss, and target levels on the chart
  • Fully adjustable in real time
  • Supports multiple measurement modes:
    • Currency (USD)
    • Points
    • Ticks
    • Percentage (%)
  • Easily modify targets and stops based on market conditions
  • Clean and non-intrusive visual layout

Why It Matters:

  • Improves risk awareness before execution
  • Reduces execution errors under pressure
  • Encourages disciplined, rule-based trading
  • Ideal for scalping and day trading CME futures

You don't manage what you can't clearly see.

Indicator 2: Market Sessions

Session-Based Market Context at a Glance

The Market Sessions indicator highlights the main global trading sessions directly on your chart, helping you understand when liquidity, volatility, and institutional participation change.

This tool allows traders to align their strategies with the correct market session instead of trading blindly throughout the day.

Key Features:

  • Displays Tokyo, London, and New York sessions
  • Visual session boxes from open to close
  • Fully configurable:
    • Custom colors
    • Adjustable session times
    • Enable/disable specific sessions
  • Works on any CME futures instrument
  • Lightweight and performance-friendly

Why It Matters:

  • Identifies high and low liquidity periods
  • Helps avoid trading during unfavorable hours
  • Improves timing and session-based strategy execution
  • Essential for futures day traders

Time is a trading variable. This indicator makes it visible.

Designed for Professional Traders

Both indicators are:

  • Built exclusively for NinjaTrader 8
  • Designed for CME futures markets
  • Focused on clarity, discipline, and execution
  • Compatible with professional risk management workflows

These are not "magic indicators" they are professional tools to support better decisions.
